About CMR AI Club

At CMR AI Club, we aim to foster innovation and learning in the field of Artificial Intelligence. Our mission is to build a thriving community of students and professionals passionate about AI, encouraging hands-on experiences and cutting-edge research.

From hosting events and workshops to developing AI projects, we are dedicated to making AI accessible to everyone. Join us and be part of a forward-thinking community driven by creativity and collaboration.
